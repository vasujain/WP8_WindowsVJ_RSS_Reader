WindowsVJ RSS Reader


WindowsVJ blog feed app for Windows Phone 8 grabs the RSS feed of popular technology blog WindowsVJ.com and displays the most recent 20 articles in tiles format. Readers can coninue reading these articles within app to the blog post.App also features Text to speech, share on social network, deep pinning features.
